This project aims to implement a traveling salesman algorithm using a genetic algorithm. 
You can assume there is a direct route connecting every pair of cities and that no two distances are equal. For cross-over to work, the route taken between cities is represented as 'nth closest unvisited city'. This way crossover can take place and no city is visited twice. Mutations occur on the rank of what city will be visited.
